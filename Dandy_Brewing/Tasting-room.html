What we do

Here, at The Dandy Brewing Company we serve 20 taps of Exceptional Craft Beer, Fine drinking food, Cocktails and Wine. We are located in the heart of Ramsay. Dandy's is famous for incredible Beer which we offer to our customer. We curated our beers as per the tastebuds of our beloved customers. We are also rated as one of the best new restaurant of 2018 in the Calgary Herald. One of the top quality Wines alongwith Food and Snacks are provided here at Dandy's. Our Chef is one of the most well known in the Restaurant industry with a great experience.
